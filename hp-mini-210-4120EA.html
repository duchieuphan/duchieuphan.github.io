Laptop HP Mini 210-4120EA review

Laptop HP Mini 110-3000 is a laptop fits for the one who takes appearance not too important. It has the price of 290.07 $ including VAT, however the Windows 7 is included which makes it stand out from other netbook standards.

With this quite acceptable price, Laptop HP Mini 110-3000 owns some spectacular features. The keyboards make the users quite comfortable when using due to the fact that the keys are seperated nicely even. The touchpad is smooth and can be replaced the mouse well. Users can work well with the help of ports: the Mini 110 has an SD/MMC slot, three USB 2 sockets and even Bluetooth.

Regarding the basic graphics of Laptop HP Mini 110-3000, this feature does not quite compatible for gaming.
